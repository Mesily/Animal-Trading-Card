Jonathan the Tortoise

by: Ani Mercy

Jonathan

The oldest living animal; born in 1832, and taken to Saint Helena from the Seychelles in the Indian Ocean in 1882. He was named Jonathan by the Saint Helena's Governor in the 1930s

Name: Jonathan

Age: 191years

Specie: Seychelles giant tortoise

Abode: Saint Helena Governor's official residence - Plantation House

Favourite meals: cabbage, cucumbers, carrots, lettuce hearts and apples

Hobbies:Sleeping; eating and mating

Interesting Facts:

  • Jonathan is blind and has no sense of smell, but his hearing is great
  • Jonathan is gay; he mates with his male companion, Fred.
  • Jonathan had his first bath at the age of 184
  • His official birthday is Dec 4th, same day as the Christmas Market at Plantation House
